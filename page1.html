Overcoming Process Hurdles

The IBC 2016 framework presents a number of operational challenges for Insolvency Professionals. Our platform is designed to empower IPs for the best case results. 

Present Challenges

Insolvency Professionals are under constant scrutiny by regulatory authorities and the Committee of Creditors to meet the process requirements proficiently. 

Our survey enlists Timeline Compliance, Soliciting Resolution Plans, Managing Company as a Going Concern and Stakeholder Communication as the top challenges faced by Insolvency Professionals in 2018.

Our technological solutions are targeted at these key hurdles that enable IPs to perform at their optimal and significantly improve the chances of a resolution.
