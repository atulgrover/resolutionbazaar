Website Regulatory Requirements

IBC mandates the presence of Corporate Debtor Website in the following section.

  1. Regulation 6 - Public announcement: To be published by the IP on the WEBSITE of the Corporate Debtor
  2. Regulation 13 - Verification of claims: The list of creditors shall be displayed on the WEBSITE of the Corporate Debtor
  3. Regulation 20 - Service of notice of meeting: The RP shall give web address to access meeting information on the WEBSITE of the Corporate Debtor
  4. Regulation 36(A) - Invitation for EOI: The RP shall publish Form G on the WEBSITE of the Corporate Debtor
  5. Regulation 36(A)(4)(c): RP to provide information about the corporate debtor as may be required by a prospective resolution applicant - INVESTMENT TEASER of the Corporate Debtor
